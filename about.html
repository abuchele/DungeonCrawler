    About Us

    This game was created by Anna Buchele, Justin Kunimune, Leon Lam, and Maxamilian Wei as part of their final project in Software Design, spring semester of 2016. All four of them were first-year students at the Olin College of Engineering, and were assigned to develop a complex piece of software that made use of object-oriented programming and python. They formed this idea based on a shared interest in rogue-likes, story, terrain generation, and games.
